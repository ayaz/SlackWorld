Copying License

We choose to apply exactly the same copyright policy as the one used by the Linux Gazette.
Namely:

  • Unless otherwise stated in the article, the copyrights of all articles and/or posts are held by their respective authors and are distributed under the Open Publication License (OPL). The OPL also applies to The Slack World as a whole.
  • There are no "license options" (additional conditions) as described in section VI of the OPL. Understand that if you republish an article or a modified/derived version, you may not impose additional restrictions on its distribution without explicit permission from the author. We believe this is consistent with the intent of the OPL, not an additional condition.
  • The author, being the copyright holder, is not bound by the license, and is free to republish the article/post or allow it to be republished under any license desired.
